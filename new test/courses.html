Embedded Systems

An embedded system is a combination of hardware and software designed to perform a specific function or set of functions within a larger system. Unlike general-purpose computers, embedded systems are specialized and optimized for particular tasks, often operating under real-time constraints.

Circuit design

Circuit design is the process of creating an electrical or electronic circuit to achieve a specific function. It involves defining the components, connections, and layout necessary to implement the desired operation of the circuit. Circuit design is a fundamental part of electronics and engineering, used to build devices ranging from simple flashlights to complex computing systems.

Industrial automation

Industrial automation is the use of control systems, such as computers, robotics, and information technologies, to handle industrial processes and machinery with minimal human intervention. Its primary goal is to enhance productivity, safety, and efficiency while reducing operational costs.
